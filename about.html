About Newfound Living

Newfound Living is a Newfoundland-owned rental company built on trust, hard work, and genuine care for both tenants and the homes we offer.

Founded by Ryan Parsons, a local homeowner with a long-time interest in real estate, Newfound Living started with a simple goal — to provide clean, comfortable, and dependable homes that people are proud to live in.

We’re not a property management company. We own every property we rent. That means tenants deal directly with the owner — no middlemen, no confusion, and no delays. When you rent with Newfound Living, you’re renting from someone who cares about the home and the people living in it.

Our Story

What began as a small renovation project grew into a genuine passion for real estate and building long-term value through responsible ownership. Over time, Newfound Living has expanded to include several rental homes across St. John’s, Mount Pearl, and nearby communities.

Each home has been carefully updated — from new flooring and modern kitchens to renovated bathrooms and energy-efficient improvements — all with the goal of creating spaces that feel safe, comfortable, and ready to call home.

Our approach is simple: take care of the homes, take care of the people, and everything else tends to fall into place.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide tenants with modern, affordable, and well-maintained homes while reinvesting in our community through thoughtful improvements and local ownership.

We believe renting should be straightforward and positive — built on respect, communication, and fairness. Every decision we make, from upgrades to lease terms, reflects that belief.

Our Values

  • Quality: We take pride in offering clean, updated homes that are properly cared for.
  • Honesty: Clear leases, open communication, and no hidden surprises.
  • Responsiveness: Repairs and maintenance are handled quickly and personally.
  • Community: We live here, we invest here, and we care about keeping neighborhoods strong.
  • Growth: As we expand, our focus stays on quality over quantity — making sure every tenant feels valued.

What We Offer

  • Clean, updated rental homes in great neighborhoods
  • Washer/dryer and modern appliances in every unit
  • Fair, transparent rental agreements
  • Direct communication with the property owner
  • Flexible options for families, professionals, and students
  • Ongoing maintenance and upgrades to keep homes comfortable and efficient

Why Choose Newfound Living

Because we treat tenants like neighbors, not numbers.

When you rent from Newfound Living, you can count on a landlord who takes pride in the homes and genuinely wants you to enjoy living there. We believe great rentals come from great relationships — built on respect, reliability, and good Newfoundland hospitality.
